Bayesian Product Discovery

Dashboards don’t change minds. This tool supports the article’s thesis: use Bayes as a formal mechanism for updating belief from a skeptical outside-view prior as high-integrity signals arrive.

Each “test” is a binary signal with an observed outcome: positive \(E^+\) or negative \(E^-\). You enter its sensitivity \(P(E^+\mid H)\) and specificity \(P(E^-\mid \neg H)\). (False negative and false positive rates are implied.)
